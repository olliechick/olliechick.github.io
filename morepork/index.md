# Morepork

Morepork is a side-scrolling game where you control a morepork, avoiding obstacles.

You are controlling Porky, a morepork who needs to get home to his family. He needs your help to get there without crashing into the various hazards in the forest!

You control Porky by shouting or holding your hand over the proximity sensor.

You can download it on 
[Google Play here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=nz.co.olliechick.morepork).

The source code can be found at [github.com/olliechick/morepork](https://github.com/olliechick/morepork).
