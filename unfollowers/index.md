# Unfollowers by Ollie

Unfollowers by Ollie is designed to keep track of who unfollows you on Instagram.
It will periodically check if you have any new unfollowers, and notify you.
You can add whichever accounts you want without signing in to them.

You can customise it to check every 30 minutes, hour, 12 hours, or 24 hours.

You can download it on 
[Google Play here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=me.olliechick.instagramunfollowers).

The source code can be found at [github.com/olliechick/unfollowers](https://github.com/olliechick/unfollowers).
