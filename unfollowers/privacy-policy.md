# Privacy policy for Unfollowers

## Information collected

Unfollowers collects and stores locally to your phone your Instagram username and password.
It also stores the list of accounts you add, and each account's followers at each time the data is downloaded 
(either manually by the user or automatically periodically). 
It will also store additional information provided by Instagram about these accounts.

It will not share this information outside your device, except to Instagram's servers through the API Instagram4Android.

## Children under 13

Instagram requires that users be at least 13 years old, so Unfollowers is not intended for children under 13.
